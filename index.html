My Hobbies

I then got a scholarship for the TechTrack to learn web development. Now I know C, Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Git.

Some books that I have read and recommend are Eragon, The Lord of the Rings, and Mistborn. These books will give you a great feel for the genre.

    • Video games are more popular then ever, and I too have been sucked into this virtual world. Gaming is a part of the now, and this is part of the reason I wanted to program so bad.
